Senior Full-Stack Software Engineer

Engineering Department | Full-Time | Remote/Hybrid

The Opportunity

We are seeking a seasoned **Senior Full-Stack Engineer** with a strong focus on backend systems and architecture. You will play a crucial role in designing, developing, and maintaining scalable web applications and microservices that power our core business offerings.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead the design and implementation of new backend APIs using **Python (Django/Flask)** or **Node.js**.
  • Collaborate with product managers and designers to translate requirements into technical specifications.
  • Optimize application for maximum speed, scalability, and security.
  • Mentor junior engineers and champion best practices in code quality, testing, and deployment (CI/CD).
  • Manage database architecture (PostgreSQL, MongoDB) and ensure efficient data storage and retrieval.

Qualifications & Skills

  • 5+ years of professional software development experience.
  • Expert proficiency in at least one modern backend framework (e.g., Django, Express, Spring Boot).
  • Strong experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP).
  • Familiarity with modern frontend frameworks (React, Vue, or Angular).
  • Proven experience with relational and NoSQL databases.

Technologies We Use:

Python Node.js React AWS Lambda Docker PostgreSQL
